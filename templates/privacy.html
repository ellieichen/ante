Privacy

Last updated: 2026-05-27

ANTE is a walk-in bidding platform. When you place a bid, you provide a small amount of information so we can run the bid, notify you if you win, and authorize a hold on your card. This page explains what we collect and what we do with it.

What we collect

  • Your name, email, and phone number, which you enter on the bid form.
  • Your bid amount and the venue you're bidding at.
  • A cookie tied to your bidding session, used only to recognize you when you return to your own bid status page.

We do not see or store your full card details. Card information is sent directly from your browser to Stripe, our payment processor, which handles authorization and capture. See Stripe's privacy policy.

How we use it

  • To run the bid: rank bids, determine winners, release holds for non-winners.
  • To text you a one-time SMS if you win, asking you to confirm or release your table.
  • To capture the bid amount as credit toward your bill if you win and confirm.
  • To respond if you contact us about your bid.

SMS messages

By providing your phone number on the bid form, you agree to receive a one-time SMS notification from ANTE about that bid. You can reply STOP at any time to opt out. Standard message and data rates may apply.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell or share your information with marketers.
  • We do not text you about anything other than the bid you placed.
  • We do not track you across other sites.

Retention

We retain bid records for our own bookkeeping. If you'd like your bid record removed, email us at the address below.
